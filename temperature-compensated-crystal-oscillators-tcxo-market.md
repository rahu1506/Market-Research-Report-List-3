<p><p>Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators (TCXOs) are electronic devices used in applications where a stable frequency reference is required. The market for TCXOs is divided into two main types: SMD Shape and PIN Shape. SMD Shape TCXOs are surface-mount devices that are compact and easy to integrate into circuit boards, while PIN Shape TCXOs have a pin-style design for easy installation. Both types offer temperature compensation to maintain precise frequency stability across varying environmental conditions, making them ideal for high-performance electronic systems.</p></p>
