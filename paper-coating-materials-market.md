<p><p>Paper coating materials market includes various types such as Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC), Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC), Kaolin Clay, Talc, Starch, Styrene Butadiene (SB) Latex, Titanium Dioxide, and Wax. GCC and PCC are used for improving print quality, while Kaolin Clay and Talc provide smoothness and brightness to paper. Starch is a binding agent, SB latex is a water-based coating, Titanium Dioxide enhances opacity, and Wax adds gloss and water resistance. These materials cater to different needs and preferences in the paper industry.</p></p>
