<p><p>Lead Type Multilayer Ceramic Capacitor Market consists of Radial Leaded and Axial Leaded Capacitors. Radial Leaded Capacitors have lead wires that extend out from opposite sides of the capacitor body, allowing for easy insertion onto a PCB. Axial Leaded Capacitors, on the other hand, have lead wires that come out from opposite ends of the capacitor body, providing a space-saving design suitable for applications with height restrictions. Both types offer high capacitance values and excellent performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
