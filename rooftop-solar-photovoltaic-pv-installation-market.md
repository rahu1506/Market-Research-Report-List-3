<p><p>The rooftop solar photovoltaic (PV) installation market can be divided into two main types based on the technology used: crystalline silicon PV and thin film PV. Crystalline silicon PV panels are made of silicon wafers and are more commonly used due to higher efficiency and durability. On the other hand, thin film PV panels are less efficient but more flexible and lightweight, making them suitable for certain applications. Both types of PV systems are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial rooftop installations.</p></p>
