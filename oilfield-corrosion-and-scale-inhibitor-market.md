<p><p>Oilfield corrosion and scale inhibitor market types include inorganic phosphate, organophosphorus compounds, polymer scale inhibitor, and others. Inorganic phosphate inhibitors work by forming a protective layer on metal surfaces, preventing corrosion. Organophosphorus compounds inhibit scale formation by binding to metal ions. Polymer scale inhibitors prevent scale deposition by dispersing particles in the water. Other inhibitors may include biocides or oxygen scavengers. Each type of inhibitor plays a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of oilfield equipment and infrastructure.</p></p>
