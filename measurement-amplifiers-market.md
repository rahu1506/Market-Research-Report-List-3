<p><p>Measurement amplifiers are specialized amplifiers used for accurate and reliable measurements in various industries. Differential measurement amplifiers are designed to measure the difference between two input signals, providing high common-mode rejection and minimizing noise in the measurements. Noise measurement amplifiers are specifically designed to measure and analyze noise signals, providing high sensitivity and low noise floor. Both types of measurement amplifiers play a crucial role in ensuring precise and trustworthy data collection in applications such as medical devices, automotive, industrial machinery, and scientific research.</p></p>
