<p><p>Panthenol market can be divided into two main types - D-Panthenol and DL-Panthenol. D-Panthenol is the biologically active form of vitamin B5, which is widely used in skin care and hair care products for its moisturizing and soothing properties. DL-Panthenol is a racemic mixture of D-Panthenol and L-Panthenol, commonly used in pharmaceuticals and animal feed. Both forms of Panthenol are popular ingredients in cosmetics and personal care products due to their beneficial effects on skin and hair.</p></p>
