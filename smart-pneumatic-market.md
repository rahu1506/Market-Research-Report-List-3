<p><p>The Smart Pneumatic Market consists of three main types: Valves, Actuators, and Modules. Valves are responsible for regulating the flow of air or gas within pneumatic systems. Actuators are components that convert electrical signals or compressed air into mechanical motion, allowing for precise control of movement. Modules are integrated systems that combine valves and actuators for a more streamlined and efficient operation. Together, these components work together to create smart pneumatic systems that offer advanced functionality and automation in various industries.</p></p>
