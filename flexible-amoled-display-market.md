<p><strong>The Flexible AMOLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible AMOLED display market consists of two main types: active-matrix organic light-emitting diode (AMOLED) displays and passive matrix OLED (PMOLED) displays. AMOLED displays use a thin film transistor backplane to control each individual pixel, allowing for higher resolution and better power efficiency. PMOLED displays use a simpler control scheme, resulting in lower cost but also lower resolution and performance. Both types are utilized in various electronic devices such as smartphones, tablets, and wearables.</p></p>
