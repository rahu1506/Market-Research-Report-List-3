<p><p>Corrugated pipe market consists of two main types: single wall corrugated pipe and double wall corrugated pipe. Single wall corrugated pipes are lighter in weight and more cost-effective, making them ideal for non-pressure applications such as drainage and sewer systems. Double wall corrugated pipes, on the other hand, are more durable and provide better structural strength, making them suitable for applications requiring higher levels of protection and longevity, such as highway culverts and stormwater management systems.</p></p>
