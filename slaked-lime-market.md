<p><p>Slaked lime, also known as calcium hydroxide, is a chemical compound that is produced by adding water to quicklime (calcium oxide). It is commonly used in various industries such as construction, agriculture, and water treatment due to its properties of being alkaline and having high reactivity. In the construction industry, slaked lime is used as a mortar additive for improving workability and performance. In agriculture, it is used to neutralize acidity in soils and improve nutrient uptake in plants. In water treatment, it is used for pH adjustment and coagulation purposes.</p><p>The global slaked lime market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
