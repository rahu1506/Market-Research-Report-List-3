<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Restaurant POS Systems can be categorized into two main types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based POS systems store data and software in the cloud, allowing for easy access from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, On-premises POS systems store data and software on-site, requiring physical servers and regular maintenance. While Cloud-based systems offer flexibility and scalability, On-premises systems provide greater control and security. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the restaurant industry.</p></p>
