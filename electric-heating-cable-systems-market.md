<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Electric Heating Cable</li><li>Constant Wattage Electric Heating Cable</li><li>Mineral Insulated Electric Heating Cable</li><li>Skin-Effect Electric Heating Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric heating cable systems are available in various types to suit different applications. Self-regulating electric heating cables adjust their heat output based on the surrounding temperature, making them energy-efficient. Constant wattage electric heating cables provide a consistent amount of heat regardless of temperature changes. Mineral insulated electric heating cables are highly durable and can withstand high temperatures. Skin-effect electric heating cables are designed for applications where high heat output is required in a compact space. Each type caters to specific heating needs in various industries.</p></p>
