<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle DC-DC Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buck Converter</li><li>Boost Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle DC-DC Converter market consists of two main types: Buck Converter and Boost Converter. Buck Converter is a type of DC-DC converter that steps down the input voltage to a lower output voltage, making it suitable for applications where the output voltage needs to be lower than the input voltage. Boost Converter, on the other hand, steps up the input voltage to a higher output voltage, making it ideal for applications where the output voltage needs to be higher than the input voltage.</p></p>
