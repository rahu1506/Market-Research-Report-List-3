<p><p>AC Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) are categorized into different types based on their tripping characteristics. Type B MCBs are mainly used for domestic applications, offering protection against overload and short circuits. Type C MCBs are suitable for commercial and industrial settings, providing higher sensitivity to fluctuations and surges. Type D MCBs are designed for heavy-duty industrial applications where equipment with high inrush currents are used. Other MCB types may include type P, type Z, and type A, each serving specific protection needs in various applications.</p></p>
