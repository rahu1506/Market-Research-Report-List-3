<p><strong>The Video Display Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Video Shifters</li><li>Video Interface Controllers</li><li>Video Coprocessors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video Display Controllers market can be divided into different types based on their functionalities. Video Shifters are used to convert video signals from one format to another. Video Interface Controllers manage the communication between the microprocessor and the display device. Video Coprocessors offload complex video processing tasks from the main processor. Other types of controllers may include video scalers, video encoders, and video decoders. Each type plays a specific role in ensuring high-quality video output on display devices.</p></p>
