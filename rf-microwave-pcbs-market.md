<p><p>RF Microwave PCBs are classified into different types based on the material used for their construction. Some common market types include PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), Ceram (Ceramic), Hydrocarbon, and Others. PTFE PCBs offer low dielectric constant and high thermal stability. Ceram PCBs provide excellent mechanical strength and high-frequency performance. Hydrocarbon PCBs are known for their low cost and good electrical properties. Other types of PCBs may include materials like FR-4 or Rogers. Each type offers unique characteristics suited for different applications in the RF Microwave industry.</p></p>
