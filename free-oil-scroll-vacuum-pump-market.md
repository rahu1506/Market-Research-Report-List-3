<p><strong>The Free-oil Scroll Vacuum Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Scroll</li><li>Double-sided Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Free-oil scroll vacuum pump market can be categorized into two types: Single-sided scroll and double-sided scroll. Single-sided scroll pumps have one spiral element while double-sided scroll pumps have two spiral elements. Single-sided scroll pumps are more compact and have lower ultimate pressure, making them suitable for small-scale applications. On the other hand, double-sided scroll pumps offer higher pumping speeds and can handle larger volumes of gas. Both types are popular choices in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, research laboratories, and medical equipment production.</p></p>
