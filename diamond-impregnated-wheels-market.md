<p><strong>The Diamond-Impregnated Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintering Type</li><li>Electroplating</li><li>Brazing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diamond-impregnated wheels are used in various industries for grinding and cutting applications. These wheels are available in different types such as sintering type, electroplating, and brazing. Sintering type wheels are made by sintering diamond crystals with metal powders, providing high hardness and wear resistance. Electroplating involves depositing a layer of diamond on the wheel surface, offering high precision and sharpness. Brazing involves attaching diamond segments to the wheel using a brazing material, ensuring strong adhesion and durability. Each type offers unique advantages for different applications.</p></p>
