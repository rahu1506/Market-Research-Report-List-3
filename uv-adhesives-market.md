<p><p>UV adhesives market is segmented into five main types: Acrylic, Cyanoacrylate, Epoxy, Silicone, and Polyurethane. Acrylic UV adhesives are versatile and offer fast curing, making them ideal for various applications. Cyanoacrylate UV adhesives provide strong bonding on a wide range of materials. Epoxy UV adhesives are known for their high strength and chemical resistance. Silicone UV adhesives offer flexibility and excellent temperature resistance. Polyurethane UV adhesives are durable and have good impact resistance, making them suitable for demanding applications.</p></p>
