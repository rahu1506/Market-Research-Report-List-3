<p><p>Diving gloves are essential equipment for divers to protect their hands from cold water, abrasions, and cuts. There are two main types of diving gloves: dry and wet. Dry diving gloves are designed to keep the hands completely dry by sealing out water, while wet diving gloves allow water to enter but insulate the hands to keep them warm. Dry gloves are typically used in colder water conditions, while wet gloves are more suitable for warmer waters. Both types offer different levels of protection and are chosen based on the diving environment.</p></p>
