<p><p>Electrolysis Method Solid Caustic Soda Market Types include the Ion Exchange Membrane Method and Diaphragm Electrolysis Method. The Ion Exchange Membrane Method involves separating the anode and cathode compartments using ion exchange membranes, resulting in higher purity and efficiency. On the other hand, the Diaphragm Electrolysis Method utilizes a porous diaphragm to separate the anode and cathode compartments, making it suitable for lower concentration electrolysis processes. Both methods offer different advantages and applications in the production of solid caustic soda.</p></p>
