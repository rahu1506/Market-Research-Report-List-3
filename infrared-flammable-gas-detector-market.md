<p><p>In the Infrared Flammable Gas Detector market, there are two main types of detectors - Point IR Detectors and Open Path IR Detectors. Point IR Detectors are stationary devices that detect gases at a specific point in space, providing continuous monitoring in a localized area. On the other hand, Open Path IR Detectors use beams of infrared light to monitor a larger area and can detect gases across a wider range. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of industrial environments by detecting flammable gases.</p></p>
