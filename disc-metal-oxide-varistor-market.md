<p><p>Disc Metal Oxide Varistors (MOV) are categorized into Low Voltage (LV) MOV and High Voltage-Medium Voltage (HV-MV) MOV varistors based on their application voltage range. LV MOVs are designed for lower voltage applications typically up to 500V, while HV-MV MOVs are utilized in higher voltage applications ranging from 500V to several thousand volts. LV MOVs are commonly used in consumer electronics and small appliances, while HV-MV MOVs are employed in industrial equipment, power distribution systems, and other high voltage applications.</p></p>
