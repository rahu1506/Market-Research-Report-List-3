<p><p>Hydraulic torque wrench market includes two main types: Drive Hydraulic Wrench and Hollow Hydraulic Wrench. Drive Hydraulic Wrenches apply torque through a square drive, typically used for general applications. Hollow Hydraulic Wrenches have a hollow center, allowing them to fit over long bolts or studs. They are commonly utilized in applications where access is limited. Each type offers specific advantages based on the job requirements, making them popular choices in industrial settings where precise torque application is crucial.</p></p>
