<p><p>Gastric band devices are used in weight loss surgeries and come in two main types: adjustable and non-adjustable gastric bands. Adjustable gastric bands allow for changes in the size of the band after surgery, providing flexibility in tightening or loosening as needed. Non-adjustable gastric bands, on the other hand, have a fixed size that cannot be changed once implanted. Both types of gastric bands are effective in helping patients achieve weight loss by limiting the amount of food the stomach can hold.</p></p>
