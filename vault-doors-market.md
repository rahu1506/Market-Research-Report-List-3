<p><p>Vault doors are specialized security doors used in banks, vaults, and high-security facilities to protect valuable assets and confidential information. There are two primary types of vault doors based on their swing direction - out-swing and in-swing. Out-swing vault doors open outward, providing more usable space inside the vault. In-swing vault doors, on the other hand, open inward, allowing for tighter security measures by preventing access from the outside. Both types are designed to provide maximum protection and security for their intended purposes.</p></p>
