<p><ul><li>Single Cardan</li><li>Double Cardan</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive cardan universal joints are categorized into three main types - single cardan, double cardan, and others. Single cardan joints consist of one U-joint with a yoke at each end, providing flexibility but limited angle capability. Double cardan joints involve two U-joints working together, reducing vibration and improving power transfer efficiency. The "others" category encompasses specialized joints such as constant-velocity joints for smoother operation. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
