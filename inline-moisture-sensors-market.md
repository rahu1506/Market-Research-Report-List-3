<p><p>Inline moisture sensors are used in various industries for measuring and controlling moisture levels in materials. There are two main types of sensors: Contact Type and Non-contact Type. Contact Type sensors physically touch the material being measured to determine moisture content, while Non-contact Type sensors use technologies such as infrared or microwave to measure moisture levels without touching the material. Both types of sensors have their own set of advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
