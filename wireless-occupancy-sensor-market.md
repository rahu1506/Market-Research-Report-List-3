<p><p>Wireless Occupancy Sensor market is divided into different types based on how they detect occupancy. Passive Infrared sensors detect heat emitted by humans, while Ultrasonic sensors emit high frequency sound waves to detect motion. Dual Technology sensors combine both PIR and Ultrasonic technologies for more accurate detection. Other types include microwave sensors and camera-based sensors. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, catering to different needs in the market for efficient and reliable occupancy detection.</p></p>
