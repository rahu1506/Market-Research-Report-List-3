<p><p>The optics light sources market includes LED and laser diode sources. LEDs are semiconductor devices that emit light when an electrical current passes through them, providing energy-efficient and long-lasting lighting solutions. Laser diodes, on the other hand, use a stimulated emission process to produce coherent and focused light beams, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and high intensity. Both LED and laser diodes are widely used in various industries such as telecommunications, medical devices, automotive, and entertainment.</p></p>
