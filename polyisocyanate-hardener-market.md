<p><p>Polyisocyanate hardeners are used in the coatings, adhesives, and sealants industries to improve the performance and durability of the final product. Two common types of polyisocyanate hardeners are TDI (toluene diisocyanate) and HDI (hexamethylene diisocyanate). TDI polyisocyanate hardeners are known for their fast curing properties and high reactivity, making them suitable for applications requiring a quick drying time. On the other hand, HDI polyisocyanate hardeners offer excellent chemical resistance and durability, making them ideal for demanding environments.</p></p>
