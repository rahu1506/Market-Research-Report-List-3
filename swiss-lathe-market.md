<p><p>The Swiss lathe market consists of two main types: CNC and conventional. CNC Swiss lathes are equipped with computer numerical control technology, allowing for precise and automated machining processes. These machines are commonly used in high-precision industries such as aerospace and medical. On the other hand, conventional Swiss lathes are operated manually and rely on the expertise and skill of the operator to control the machining process. They are typically used in smaller-scale production settings where flexibility and customization are key.</p></p>
