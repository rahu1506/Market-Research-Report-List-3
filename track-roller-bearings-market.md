<p><p>Track roller bearings come in two main types based on their outer ring structures: with threaded bearings and with non-threaded bearings. Threaded bearings have a threaded shaft attached to the outer ring, which allows for easy installation and adjustment. On the other hand, non-threaded bearings do not have a threaded shaft, making them more suitable for applications where a fixed position is required. Both types offer reliable support and guide motion in various industrial machinery and equipment.</p></p>
