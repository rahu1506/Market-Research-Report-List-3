<p><p>Thermal Fuse Metal Oxide Varistors (TMOV) are a type of protective device used in electronic circuits to prevent damage from overvoltage. They come in three market types based on the number of lead wires: 2 lead wires, 3 lead wires, and 5 lead wires. The 2 lead wire TMOV is the most basic, offering protection for single-phase circuits. The 3 lead wire TMOV is more advanced, providing protection for both single and three-phase circuits. The 5 lead wire TMOV is the most comprehensive, offering enhanced protection and diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
