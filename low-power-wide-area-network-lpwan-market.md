<p><p>LPWAN refers to a type of wireless network technology designed for long-range communication with low power consumption, making it suitable for Internet of Things applications. The market is dominated by four main types: SIGFOX, LoRaWAN, Weightless, and NB-IoT. SIGFOX uses ultra-narrowband technology, LoRaWAN operates on unlicensed spectrum, Weightless is designed for machine-to-machine communication, and NB-IoT is a cellular technology. Other LPWAN options include Symphony Link, RPMA, and LTE-M. Each type offers varying levels of performance and coverage for different IoT use cases.</p></p>
