<p><p>FTTx equipment market is categorized into various types based on the extent of fiber optic network deployment. FTTN, or Fiber To the Node/Neighborhood, brings fiber optic cables to a neighborhood node which serves multiple households. FTTE, or Fiber to the Exchange, connects fiber optic cables directly to a central exchange or building. FTTR, or Fiber To the Remote Terminal, involves extending fiber optic cables to remote terminals or locations. Other markets in FTTx equipment include solutions for specific industry verticals such as healthcare, education, and government.</p></p>
