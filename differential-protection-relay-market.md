<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Differential Protection Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Differential Relay</li><li>Biased or Percentage Differential Relay</li><li>Voltage Balance Differential Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential protection relays are used in power systems to provide protection against internal faults by comparing the currents or voltages at different points in the system. Current differential relays compare the input and output currents to detect faults. Biased or percentage differential relays use a bias or percentage setting to account for differences in current transformer ratios. Voltage balance differential relays compare the voltages at both ends of a protected zone to detect faults. Each type of differential relay offers unique advantages for specific applications in power system protection.</p></p>
