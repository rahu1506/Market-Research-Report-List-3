<p><p>Optical modulators are devices that manipulate the intensity, phase, or polarization of light signals. There are two main types of optical modulators materials market: fiber-coupled optical modulators materials and free-space optical modulators materials. Fiber-coupled modulators are designed for use in fiber optic communication systems, while free-space modulators are used in applications where light signals are transmitted through open air. Both types of modulators play a crucial role in various technologies such as telecommunications, laser systems, and optical sensing.</p></p>
