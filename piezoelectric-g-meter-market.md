<p><p>Piezoelectric G meters are devices that measure acceleration, also known as G-force. The market for these meters can be classified into 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis types based on the number of axes they measure. </p><p>1-axis meters measure acceleration in one direction, typically either vertical or horizontal. 2-axis meters measure acceleration in two perpendicular directions. 3-axis meters measure acceleration in all three axes, providing a complete picture of movement in three-dimensional space. Each type of meter offers different levels of accuracy and precision in measuring G-force.</p></p>
