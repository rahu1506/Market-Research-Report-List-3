<p><p>FPC stands for Flexible Printed Circuit, which is commonly used in power battery applications. Single layer FPCs are thin and flexible circuits with a single conductive layer, suitable for simple designs. Double layer FPCs consist of two conductive layers, allowing for more complex circuit designs. Multi-layer FPCs have three or more conductive layers, providing even greater design flexibility and functionality. Each type of FPC offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the power battery application.</p></p>
