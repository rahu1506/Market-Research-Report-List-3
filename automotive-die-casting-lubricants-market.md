<p><strong>The Automotive Die Casting Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Lubricants</li><li>Plunger Lubricants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive die casting lubricants can be categorized into three main types: die lubricants, plunger lubricants, and others. Die lubricants are used to minimize friction and protect the mold surface during the casting process. Plunger lubricants are specifically designed for lubricating the plunger that forces the molten metal into the die cavity. The "others" category includes specialty lubricants that serve various purposes in the die casting process, such as reducing wear and prolonging the lifespan of the equipment. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring a smooth and efficient die casting operation.</p></p>
