<p><p>Manganese Bronze is commonly used in various industries due to its excellent mechanical properties and corrosion resistance. There are two main types in the market - QMn1.5 (Cu-1.5Mn) and QMn5 (Cu-5Mn). QMn1.5 contains 1.5% manganese, while QMn5 contains 5% manganese. QMn1.5 is preferred for applications requiring higher strength and hardness, while QMn5 is suitable for applications where improved wear resistance is needed. Both types are widely used in manufacturing components for marine, automotive, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
