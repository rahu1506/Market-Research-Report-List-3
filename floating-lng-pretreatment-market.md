<p><p>Floating LNG pretreatment market includes various types such as Amine Treating, Membrane Treating, Mercury Removal, and Oxygen Removal. Amine Treating involves the use of chemicals to remove acidic gases like carbon dioxide and hydrogen sulfide. Membrane Treating utilizes membrane technology to separate impurities from natural gas. Mercury Removal is the process of extracting mercury compounds from the gas stream. Oxygen Removal involves techniques to eliminate oxygen from the gas to prevent corrosion and equipment damage. These different types cater to specific needs and requirements in the floating LNG industry.</p></p>
