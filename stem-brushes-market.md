<p><p>Stem brushes come in different types based on the number of rows of bristles they have. 1-Row stem brushes have a single row of bristles, providing precise cleaning for small or intricate areas. Half-row stem brushes have bristles only on one side, making them suitable for cleaning tight spaces from one angle. 2-Row stem brushes have two parallel rows of bristles, offering more coverage and efficiency in cleaning larger surfaces. Each type caters to specific cleaning needs, providing varying levels of effectiveness and versatility.</p></p>
