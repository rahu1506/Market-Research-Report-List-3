<p><p>The Tibia External Fixation System market consists of two main types: Tubular and Circular. The Tubular market refers to external fixation systems that utilize straight rods or tubes to stabilize the fracture site. On the other hand, the Circular market involves external fixation systems that use circular rings or frames connected by wires to stabilize the fracture. Both types offer various advantages and are used depending on the specific needs of the patient and the complexity of the fracture.</p></p>
