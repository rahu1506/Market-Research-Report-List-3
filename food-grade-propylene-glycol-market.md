<p><p>Food grade propylene glycol is available in three main types: Mono Propylene Glycol (MPG), Dipropylene Glycol (DPG), and Tripropylene Glycol (TPG). MPG is the most commonly used type and is known for its hygroscopic properties, making it ideal for food preservation and flavoring applications. DPG is often used as a solvent in food processing, while TPG is less common and is primarily used in specialty food products. Each type of propylene glycol offers unique benefits and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
