<p><strong>The Electro-Optical Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Switches</li><li>Multimode Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-Optical Switches are devices used to redirect optical signals in telecommunications networks. Single-mode switches are designed for use with single-mode optical fibers, which have a smaller core size and can transmit signals over longer distances with higher clarity. On the other hand, multimode switches are used with multimode fibers, which have a larger core size and are better suited for shorter distances. Both types of switches play a key role in ensuring efficient and reliable data transmission in optical networks.</p></p>
