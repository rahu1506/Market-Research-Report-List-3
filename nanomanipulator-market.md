<p><p>The Nanomanipulator Market consists of two main types: Electron Microscope and Scanning Probe Microscope. Electron Microscope-based nanomanipulators use beams of electrons to create images with high magnification and resolution, allowing researchers to manipulate materials at the nanoscale. On the other hand, Scanning Probe Microscope-based nanomanipulators utilize a probe to scan surfaces and manipulate objects at the atomic and molecular levels. Both types of nanomanipulators play a crucial role in various fields such as nanotechnology, material science, and biology.</p></p>
