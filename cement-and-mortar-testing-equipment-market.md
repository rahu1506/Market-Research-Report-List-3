<p><p>Cement and Mortar Testing Equipment market includes various types of machinery used for testing the quality and strength of cement and mortar. Compression machines are used to determine the compressive strength of these materials. Humidity cabinets are used to test the effects of moisture on cement samples. Tensile testing machines measure the tensile strength of the materials. Blaine apparatus is used to measure the fineness of cement. Cement autoclaves are used for accelerated testing of cement durability. Bond strength testers determine the adhesion strength of mortar to other materials.</p></p>
