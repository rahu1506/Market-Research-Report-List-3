<p><p>Board mount transformers are commonly categorized into different types based on their applications. Audio transformers are designed for transferring audio signals between circuits with minimal distortion. Autotransformers are used for stepping-up or stepping-down voltage levels in a single winding. Flyback transformers are used in switch mode power supplies to store and transfer energy efficiently. Other variations of board mount transformers include pulse transformers, isolation transformers, and power transformers. Each type is tailored for specific circuit requirements and plays a crucial role in various electronic applications.</p></p>
