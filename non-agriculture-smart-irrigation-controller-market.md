<p><strong>The Non-Agriculture Smart Irrigation Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weather-based Controllers</li><li>Sensor-based Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weather-based controllers in the non-agriculture smart irrigation controller market use real-time weather data to adjust watering schedules based on current environmental conditions. This helps prevent overwatering or underwatering, saving water and optimizing plant health. Sensor-based controllers, on the other hand, rely on sensors placed in the soil to monitor moisture levels and only water when necessary. These controllers provide precise and customized irrigation, reducing water waste and promoting efficient plant growth. Both types offer innovative solutions for sustainable water management in non-agricultural settings.</p></p>
