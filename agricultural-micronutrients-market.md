<p><p>Agricultural micronutrients market types include zinc, copper, boron, iron, manganese, and molybdenum. Zinc is essential for plant growth and development. Copper helps in enzyme production and energy transfer in plants. Boron aids in cell wall formation and pollination. Iron is crucial for chlorophyll synthesis and photosynthesis. Manganese plays a key role in nitrogen metabolism and formation of proteins. Molybdenum is necessary for nitrogen fixation in legumes. These micronutrients are crucial for healthy plant growth and crop production.</p></p>
