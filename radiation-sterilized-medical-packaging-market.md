<p><p>Radiation sterilized medical packaging includes various types such as thermoform trays, sterile bottles & containers, vials & ampoules, pre-fillable inhalers, sterile closures, pre-filled syringes, blister & clamshells, bags & pouches, and wraps. Thermoform trays are used for organizing and protecting medical devices, while sterile bottles and containers are used for liquid medications. Vials and ampoules are commonly used for storing injectable medications, while pre-fillable inhalers are used for respiratory drugs. Sterile closures ensure the integrity of the packaging, while blister packs and clamshells are used for individual doses. Bags, pouches, and wraps provide additional protection for medical devices and supplies.</p></p>
