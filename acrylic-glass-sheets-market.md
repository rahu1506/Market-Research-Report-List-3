<p><p>Acrylic glass sheets come in two main types: Extruded Acrylic Sheet and Cast Acrylic Sheet. Extruded acrylic sheets are manufactured using a continuous production process, resulting in a more uniform thickness and a lower cost compared to cast acrylic sheets. On the other hand, cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, allowing for more intricate shapes and better optical clarity. Both types are widely used in various industries such as construction, signage, and furniture due to their durability, weather resistance, and versatility.</p></p>
