<p><p>Fire suppression products are categorized into different types based on the method of extinguishing fires. The dry chemical fire suppression market involves the use of powder or chemical agents to smother fires. Foam fire suppression uses a foam blanket to suppress flames and cool the fire. CO2 fire suppression releases carbon dioxide gas to displace oxygen and extinguish the fire. Other types include water mist, clean agent, and inert gas suppression systems, each offering unique features and benefits in fire suppression applications.</p></p>
