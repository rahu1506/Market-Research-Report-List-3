<p><p>IC substrate material market consists of various types such as substrate resin, copper foil, insulation materials, drill, and other components. Substrate resin is used as a base material for semiconductors, while copper foils provide electrical conductivity. Insulation materials prevent electrical interference, while drills create holes for circuit connections. Other components include adhesives, films, and coatings that enhance the performance and durability of IC substrates. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall functionality and efficiency of integrated circuits.</p></p>
