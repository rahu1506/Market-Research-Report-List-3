<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household range hoods are classified into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I range hoods are designed for use over residential cooking equipment that produce grease-laden vapors. They feature a grease extraction filter and are suitable for heavy-duty cooking. Type II range hoods are used in residential kitchens where the cooking processes produce only steam, light smoke, and moisture. They typically feature aluminum mesh filters for grease and odor removal. Both Type I and Type II range hoods play a crucial role in maintaining indoor air quality and safety in households.</p></p>
