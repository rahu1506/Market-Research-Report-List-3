<p><p>Nanofiltration elements market includes two main types: Spiral Membrane and Tubular Membrane. Spiral Membrane elements are known for their compact design, easy installation, and cost-effectiveness. They are commonly used in water treatment processes and industrial applications. Tubular Membrane elements, on the other hand, provide higher filtration efficiency and durability compared to spiral membranes. They are suitable for high-pressure filtration processes and are often utilized in pharmaceutical, food and beverage, and chemical industries. Both types play a crucial role in the nanofiltration market, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
