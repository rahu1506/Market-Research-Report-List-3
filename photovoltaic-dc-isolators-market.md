<p><ul><li>2 Pole</li><li>4 Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photovoltaic DC Isolators market offers two main types: 2 Pole and 4 Pole isolators. 2 Pole isolators provide a simple and cost-effective solution for DC disconnection in photovoltaic systems. On the other hand, 4 Pole isolators offer additional safety features by disconnecting both positive and negative conductors simultaneously. While 2 Pole isolators are suitable for smaller installations, 4 Pole isolators are recommended for larger systems where enhanced safety is required. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe operation and maintenance of photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
