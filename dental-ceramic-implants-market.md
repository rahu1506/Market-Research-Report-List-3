<p><p>One Piece Ceramic Implants are designed as a single unit, with the implant and abutment combined. This type of implant offers simplicity and strength. Two Piece Ceramic Implants consist of a separate implant and abutment that are connected during the procedure. This design allows for customization and flexibility in matching the patient's needs. Both types of ceramic implants offer biocompatibility and natural aesthetics, making them a popular choice in the dental implant market for patients seeking a metal-free option.</p></p>
