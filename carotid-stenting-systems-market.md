<p><p>Carotid stenting systems are classified into three main types: Bare Metal Stents, Polymers, and Others. Bare Metal Stents are metal tubes used to treat carotid artery disease by improving blood flow. Polymers are biocompatible materials that can be used to coat stents, reducing the risk of restenosis. Other types of carotid stenting systems may include drug-eluting stents, covered stents, or hybrid stents. Each type of system offers unique benefits and considerations when used in carotid artery interventions.</p></p>
