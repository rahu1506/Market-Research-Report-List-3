<p><strong>The Medical Polylactic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-Polylactic (PLLA)</li><li>D-Polylactic (PDLA)</li><li>DL-Polylactic (PDLLA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical polylactic acid (PLA) is a biodegradable polymer used in medical applications. There are three main types of PLA: L-Polylactic (PLLA), D-Polylactic (PDLA), and DL-Polylactic (PDLLA). PLLA is a stereoisomer with high crystallinity and mechanical strength, suitable for applications like sutures and orthopedic implants. PDLA is another stereoisomer with lower crystallinity and flexibility, used in drug delivery systems. PDLLA, a racemic mixture of PLLA and PDLA, combines the properties of both for various medical devices and implants.</p></p>
