<p><p>The Anti Peptic Ulcer Drugs market includes various types of medications that are used to treat peptic ulcers and related conditions. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) and Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB) work by reducing acid production in the stomach. Antacids neutralize stomach acid, while H2 Antagonists block histamine receptors to decrease acid production. Antibiotics are used to treat H. pylori infections, a common cause of peptic ulcers. Ulcer Protective Drugs help to protect the stomach lining from damage caused by acid.</p></p>
