<p><p>Poly Lactic Acid (PLA) is a biodegradable polymer with three main types: Poly(L-lactic)acid, Poly(D-lactic)acid, and Poly(DL-lactic)acid. Poly(L-lactic)acid is derived from renewable resources and has good resistance to high temperatures, making it suitable for packaging and medical applications. Poly(D-lactic)acid has a higher crystallinity and thermal stability compared to Poly(L-lactic)acid, making it ideal for durable products. Poly(DL-lactic)acid is a racemic mixture of both L and D forms, offering a balance of properties suitable for various applications in the market.</p></p>
