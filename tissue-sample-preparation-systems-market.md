<p><p>Tissue sample preparation systems can be categorized into two main types based on their operation mode: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic systems are fully automated, requiring minimal manual intervention for sample processing. They are preferred for high-throughput applications and reduce human errors. Semi-automatic systems, on the other hand, involve a combination of manual and automated steps in sample preparation. These systems offer more flexibility and control over the process, making them suitable for research applications that require customized protocols. Both types play a crucial role in streamlining tissue sample preparation in various scientific settings.</p></p>
