<p><p>Concentrated Photovoltaic (CPV) technology is divided into two main market types: Low Concentration Photovoltaic (LCPV) and High Concentration Photovoltaic (HCPV). LCPV systems use lenses or mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a small area of solar cells, while HCPV systems use more sophisticated optics to achieve even higher levels of concentration. Both types offer higher efficiency compared to traditional PV systems, although HCPV systems require more precise tracking and cooling mechanisms due to the increased concentration levels.</p></p>
