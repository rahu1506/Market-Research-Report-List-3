<p><p>Network cables market types such as AS-i, ATM, CC-LINK, Fieldbus, and CANopen target specific industries or applications with unique requirements. AS-i cables are used for connecting sensors and actuators in industrial automation. ATM cables support high-speed data transmission in telecommunications. CC-LINK cables are commonly used in factory automation. Fieldbus cables enable communication between field devices in industrial environments. CANopen cables are designed for networking within machines and vehicles. Each market type serves a distinct purpose in facilitating reliable and efficient data communication.</p></p>
