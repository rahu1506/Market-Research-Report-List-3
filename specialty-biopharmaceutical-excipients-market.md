<p><p>The Specialty Biopharmaceutical Excipients market includes various types such as swelling agents, solubilizers, surfactants, and others. Swelling agents are used to increase the volume of a dosage form upon contact with liquid, aiding in drug release. Solubilizers enhance the solubility of poorly soluble drugs, improving their bioavailability. Surfactants reduce the surface tension between liquids, enabling efficient mixing of hydrophobic and hydrophilic components in pharmaceutical formulations. Other excipients may include stabilizers, preservatives, and coloring agents among others to enhance the overall effectiveness and appearance of the final product.</p></p>
