Each type of enzyme plays a crucial role in soil health and fertility by influencing nutrient cycling, decomposition of organic matter, and overall biological activity. Understanding the diverse functions of these enzymes is essential for managing soil quality and promoting sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
