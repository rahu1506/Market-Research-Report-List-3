<p><p>Lavalier microphones are small, discreet microphones often used in broadcasting and public speaking. There are four main types of lavalier microphones: Omnidirectional, Cardioid, Omni, and Supercardioid. Omnidirectional microphones pick up sound equally from all directions, while Cardioid microphones focus on sound from the front and reject noise from the back. Omni microphones capture sound from all directions equally. Supercardioid microphones have a narrow pickup pattern, focusing on sound from the front while minimizing side and rear noise.</p></p>
