<p><p>Active Blind Spot Monitoring systems actively detect and alert drivers to vehicles in their blind spots through visual or audible warnings. These systems typically use radar or cameras to continuously monitor the blind spots. Passive Blind Spot Monitoring systems rely on the driver to check their blind spot, with the system providing a warning only if a vehicle is detected in the blind spot. Active systems are considered more advanced and proactive, while passive systems are more reliant on driver awareness.</p></p>
