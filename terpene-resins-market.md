<p><p>Terpene resins are divided into two main types based on their physical state: liquid type and solid type. Liquid type terpene resins are typically in the form of oils or viscous liquids and are commonly used in applications such as adhesives, coatings, and printing inks. Solid type terpene resins, on the other hand, are in a solid state and are used in products like varnishes, rubber compounding, and chewing gum. Both types of terpene resins offer unique properties and benefits depending on the application.</p></p>
