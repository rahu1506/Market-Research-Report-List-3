<p><p>Polyurethane (PU) soles are commonly used in footwear due to their durability, flexibility, and shock absorption properties. Solid PU soles are dense and provide excellent stability and abrasion resistance. Cellular PU soles have a lighter weight and better shock absorption, making them ideal for athletic shoes. Liquid PU is used for custom-made soles that mold to the wearer's foot for optimal comfort. Each type of PU sole caters to different needs and preferences in the market, offering a wide range of choices for footwear manufacturers.</p></p>
