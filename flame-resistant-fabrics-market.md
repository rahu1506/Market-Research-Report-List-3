<p><p>The flame resistant fabrics market can be categorized into three types: Inherent Flame Resistant Fabrics, Treated Flame Resistant Fabrics, and Hybrid Flame Resistant Fabrics. Inherent flame resistant fabrics are materials that have flame-resistant properties inherently built into the fabric's fibers. Treated flame resistant fabrics are regular fabrics that have been treated with special chemicals to make them flame resistant. Hybrid flame resistant fabrics are a combination of both inherent and treated fabrics, offering enhanced protection against flames and heat.</p></p>
