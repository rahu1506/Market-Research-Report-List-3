<p><p>R-C Oscillators are a type of electronic oscillator that uses a combination of resistors and capacitors to generate a continuous output signal. There are three main types of R-C Oscillators in the market: Wien Bridge Oscillator, Twin-T Oscillator, and Phase-Shift Oscillator. </p><p>The Wien Bridge Oscillator uses a bridge circuit with a Wien bridge network to generate a sine wave output. The Twin-T Oscillator utilizes a twin-T network to produce a square wave output. The Phase-Shift Oscillator employs a phase-shift network to generate a multiple-phase output.</p></p>
