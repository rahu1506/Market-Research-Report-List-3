<p><p>Long-life flavored milk refers to flavored milk products that have been treated with ultra-high-temperature processing (UHT) to extend their shelf life. These products can be stored unopened for a longer period of time compared to fresh flavored milk, making them convenient for consumers. On the other hand, fresh flavored milk is pasteurized but not ultra-pasteurized, giving it a shorter shelf life and requiring refrigeration. Fresh flavored milk is often preferred by consumers seeking a more natural and less processed option.</p></p>
