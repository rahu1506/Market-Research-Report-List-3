<p><p>Aerospace structural adhesives are classified into four main types: Film Adhesives, which are cut into specific shapes and sizes and are commonly used for bonding large surface areas; Paste Adhesives, which are typically applied with a brush or spatula and are ideal for bonding irregular surfaces; Primers, which are used to improve the adhesion of the adhesive to the substrate; and Other adhesives, which include specialty adhesives with unique properties for specific aerospace applications.
