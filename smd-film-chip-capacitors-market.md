<p><p>SMD Film Chip Capacitors are categorized into different types based on the material used for the film, including PPS Film, PET Film, PEN Film, and others. PPS Film capacitors offer excellent temperature stability and low dielectric loss, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. PET Film capacitors are known for their high insulation resistance and low moisture absorption. PEN Film capacitors provide high capacitance stability and reliability. The "Others" category includes capacitors made from different materials or with unique characteristics not covered by the other categories.</p></p>
