Isobutylene Method involves utilizing Isobutylene gas to create MMA resins, known for their excellent weather and chemical resistance. Ethylene Method utilizes Ethylene gas to produce MMA resins, offering superior adhesion and long-lasting performance. Each method caters to specific needs and preferences in the traffic paint market.</p></p>
