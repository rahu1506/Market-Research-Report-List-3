<p><p>Spray adhesives market is divided into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and hot melt. Solvent-based adhesives use chemicals to create a strong bond, making them ideal for tough, industrial applications. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and have low toxicity, making them suitable for indoor use. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, providing a quick and effective bonding solution. Each type of adhesive offers unique benefits and is used in a variety of industries for different purposes.</p></p>
