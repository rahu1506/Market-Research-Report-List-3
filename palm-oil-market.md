<p><p>Palm Oil market is divided into two main types: Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Olein. Crude Palm Oil is the raw oil extracted from palm fruit, retaining its natural red color and high levels of nutrients. It is commonly used in food products and industrial applications. Palm Olein is a refined form of CPO, processed to remove impurities and create a clear, golden liquid. It is mainly used for cooking oil due to its high heat resistance and stability. Both CPO and Palm Olein play significant roles in the global palm oil industry.</p></p>
