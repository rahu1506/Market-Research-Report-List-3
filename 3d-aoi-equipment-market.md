<p><p>Offline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment refers to systems that inspect printed circuit boards (PCBs) outside of the production line. This equipment is typically used for detailed analysis and troubleshooting. </p><p>On the other hand, Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment is installed within the production line itself. These systems provide real-time inspection of PCBs during the manufacturing process, allowing for immediate feedback and adjustments to ensure high quality and efficiency. </p><p>Both types of AOI equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and quality of PCBs in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
