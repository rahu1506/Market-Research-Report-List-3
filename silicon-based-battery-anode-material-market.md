<p><p>The silicon-based battery anode material market can be divided into two main types: SiO/C and Si/C. SiO/C consists of silicon oxide combined with carbon, which provides stability and enhances the conductivity of the material. On the other hand, Si/C combines silicon with carbon to improve the structural integrity and increase the energy storage capacity of the anode material. Both types play a crucial role in the development of high-performance silicon-based batteries for various applications.</p></p>
