<p><p>Metal stamping equipment is used in various processes such as blanking, embossing, bending, coining, and flanging. Blanking involves cutting out a flat shape from a sheet of metal. Embossing creates raised patterns on the metal surface. Bending is used to create angles and shapes in the metal. Coining involves compressing the metal to create precise designs. Flanging is a process that creates flanges or rims on the edge of the metal. Each type of stamping equipment serves a different purpose in metal fabrication.</p></p>
