<p><p>Laminar Air Flow Cabinets are available in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar flow cabinets are designed for applications where the work surface needs to be easily accessible from the front, making them ideal for tasks such as cell culture and sample preparation. Horizontal laminar flow cabinets, on the other hand, are suitable for operations where the work surface needs to be deeper, such as in microbiology or pharmaceutical applications. Both types offer a controlled, clean airflow to protect samples from contamination.</p></p>
