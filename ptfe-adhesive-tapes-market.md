<p><strong>The PTFE Adhesive Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Density</li><li>Standard Density</li><li>Medium Density</li><li>High Density</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE adhesive tapes are categorized based on their density into four main types: low density, standard density, medium density, and high density. Low density tapes are lightweight and highly flexible, making them suitable for applications requiring conformability. Standard density tapes offer a balance between flexibility and durability. Medium density tapes provide increased strength and dimensional stability. High density tapes are the most rigid and durable, ideal for applications with heavy loads or high temperatures. Each type caters to different requirements in industries such as packaging, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
