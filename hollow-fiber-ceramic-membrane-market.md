<p><p>Hollow fiber ceramic membrane market is divided into three main types: Ultrafiltration, Microfiltration, and Nanofiltration. Ultrafiltration membranes have larger pore sizes and are used for separating particles and macromolecules. Microfiltration membranes have smaller pore sizes and are used for separating bacteria and suspended solids. Nanofiltration membranes have even smaller pore sizes and are used for separating salts and small molecules. Each type of membrane caters to specific filtration needs in various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
