<p><p>Consumer MEMS microphones are classified into two main types: analog and digital. Analog MEMS microphones capture sound waves and convert them into electrical signals for processing. They are commonly used in applications where high-quality audio is essential. On the other hand, digital MEMS microphones have an onboard ADC that converts the analog signal into a digital signal for direct processing. They are popular in applications where digital data is preferred for ease of integration. Both types cater to the growing demand for smaller, more efficient microphone solutions in consumer electronics.</p></p>
