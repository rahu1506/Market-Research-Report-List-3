<p><p>The golf footwear market can be categorized into three main types: spiked or cleated golf footwear, spikeless golf footwear, and others. Spikeless golf footwear, on the other hand, offers a more versatile design that can be worn on and off the course with a nub or dimple pattern on the sole for grip. The "others" category includes unconventional golf footwear styles that may not fit into the spiked or spikeless categories.</p></p>
