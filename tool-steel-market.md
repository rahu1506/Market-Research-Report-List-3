<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Tool Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon Tool Steel</li><li>Alloy Tool Steel</li><li>High Speed Tool Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tool Steel is classified into three main types based on their composition and properties: Carbon Tool Steel, Alloy Tool Steel, and High Speed Tool Steel. </p><p>Carbon Tool Steel contains only carbon as the main alloying element, providing high strength and wear resistance. </p><p>Alloy Tool Steel contains additional alloying elements such as chromium, tungsten, and vanadium to enhance properties like hardness, toughness, and resistance to wear and deformation. </p><p>High Speed Tool Steel is specifically designed for high cutting speeds, with added elements like cobalt, tungsten, and molybdenum to maintain hardness and heat resistance at elevated temperatures.</p></p>
