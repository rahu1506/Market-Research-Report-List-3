<p><p>Medical adhesion barriers are available in various formulations such as film, gel, and liquid. Film formulation is a thin, flexible sheet applied directly to tissues to prevent adhesions post-surgery. Gel formulation is a viscous substance that can be easily applied to tissue surfaces to act as a physical barrier. Liquid formulation is a flowing substance that can be sprayed or irrigated onto tissues to prevent adhesions. Each formulation type offers unique benefits and applications in the medical adhesion barrier market.</p></p>
