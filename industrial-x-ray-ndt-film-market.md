<p><strong>The Industrial X-Ray NDT Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screen Type Films</li><li>Non-Screen Type Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-Ray NDT Film market offers two main types: Screen Type Films and Non-Screen Type Films. Screen Type Films require the use of an intensifying screen to enhance image quality, resulting in higher sensitivity. On the other hand, Non-Screen Type Films do not need a screen and provide direct exposure to X-rays, offering higher resolution. Both types cater to different industrial needs and preferences, with screen type films being more commonly used for general radiography applications and non-screen type films for high-definition imaging purposes.</p></p>
