<p><ul><li>Surface Applied Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface applied coatings in the concrete water repellent market are products that are applied directly to the surface of concrete to create a protective barrier against water penetration. These coatings can come in the form of sealants, membranes, or penetrating sealers. They work by forming a thin film on the surface of the concrete, preventing water from seeping into the pores of the material. Surface applied coatings are popular for their ease of application and effectiveness in protecting concrete structures from damage caused by water infiltration.</p></p>
