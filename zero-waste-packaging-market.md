<p><p>Zero Waste Packaging market consists of three main types: re-usable goods, compostable goods, and edible goods. Compostable goods are made from biodegradable materials that break down into compost when disposed of properly. Edible goods are packaging materials made from ingredients that are safe to consume, such as edible seaweed wraps. These alternatives aim to reduce waste and promote sustainability in packaging.</p></p>
