<p><p>Automotive Steering Angle Sensor Market Types can be broadly categorized into Digital Sensors and Analog Sensors. Digital sensors provide more accurate and precise measurements of steering angles through the use of digital signals. On the other hand, Analog sensors are based on continuous voltage signals to measure steering angles. Digital sensors offer better performance and reliability compared to analog sensors, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles. However, analog sensors are still used in some applications due to their lower cost and simplicity.</p></p>
