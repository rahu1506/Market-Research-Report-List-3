<p><p>Electro-erosion fluid, also known as dielectric fluid, is a type of specialized fluid used in electrical discharge machining (EDM) processes to help remove material from a workpiece. This fluid serves as a medium for the electrical discharge to occur between the electrode and workpiece, allowing for precise and controlled material removal.</p><p>The Electro-Erosion Fluid Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for EDM processes in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics, where precision machining is essential.
