<p><p>The Cooling Fabrics market can be categorized into two main types: Synthetic Cooling Fabrics and Natural Cooling Fabrics. On the other hand, Natural Cooling Fabrics, like cotton, linen, or bamboo, are breathable materials that offer a cooling effect through improved air circulation and moisture absorption. Both types of cooling fabrics cater to individuals seeking comfort and temperature regulation in their clothing.</p></p>
