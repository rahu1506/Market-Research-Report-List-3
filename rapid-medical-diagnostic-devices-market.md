<p><p>Rapid medical diagnostic devices market includes various types such as lateral flow, agglutination assays, flow-through, and biosensors. Lateral flow tests are simple, portable devices that provide quick results for a variety of conditions. Agglutination assays involve the clumping of particles in the presence of specific antibodies. Flow-through devices use a continuous flow system to detect analytes in a sample. Biosensors use biological components to detect specific molecules. These devices play a crucial role in providing fast and accurate diagnosis in healthcare settings.</p></p>
