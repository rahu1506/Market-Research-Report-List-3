<p><p>LED fishing lights are available in two main market types: water lights and underwater lights. Water lights are designed to be mounted above the surface of the water, attracting fish to the area by creating a bright light source. Underwater lights are submerged in the water and typically attached to a boat or dock to illuminate the water below, attracting fish to the light source. Both types of LED fishing lights are popular among anglers for increasing visibility and attracting fish during nighttime fishing trips.</p></p>
