<p><p>Saline-filled breast implants come in two main types: smooth implants and textured implants. Smooth implants have a smooth outer surface, which can move within the breast pocket, giving a natural feel and appearance. Textured implants, on the other hand, have a rough outer surface designed to adhere to the surrounding tissue, reducing the risk of implant displacement. Both types have their own benefits and considerations, and the choice between them is often based on individual patient preferences and cosmetic goals.</p></p>
