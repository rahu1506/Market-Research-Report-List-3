<p><strong>The General Purpose Plating Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Complexing Agent</li><li>Buffer Agent</li><li>Activator</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General purpose plating chemicals are categorized into different types based on their functions in the plating process. Complexing agents are used to form stable complexes with metal ions, improving the plating efficiency. Buffer agents help to maintain the pH balance of the plating solution, ensuring optimal conditions for plating. Activators enhance the adhesion of the plated metal to the substrate. Other markets in this category may include brighteners, leveling agents, or inhibitors that serve specific purposes in the plating process.</p></p>
