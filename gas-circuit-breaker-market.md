<p><ul><li>Single Interrupter</li><li>Two Interrupter</li><li>Four Interrupter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas circuit breakers are available in three main types: single interrupter, two interrupter, and four interrupter market. Single interrupter breakers have a single interrupter unit for interruption of the circuit. Two interrupter breakers have two units working together for interruption, providing increased reliability. Four interrupter breakers are designed with four interrupter units for even higher levels of reliability and performance. Each type offers different levels of interrupting capability and reliability depending on the specific requirements of the electrical system.</p></p>
