<p><strong>The Fly Ash Bricks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F Fly Ash Bricks</li><li>Class C Fly Ash Bricks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fly Ash Bricks are divided into two main types based on the type of fly ash used in their production. Class F Fly Ash Bricks are made using fly ash with low calcium oxide content, while Class C Fly Ash Bricks are made using fly ash with high calcium oxide content. Class F bricks are more commonly used due to their higher strength and durability, while Class C bricks are preferred for their self-cementing properties, making them suitable for construction in areas with high groundwater levels.</p></p>
