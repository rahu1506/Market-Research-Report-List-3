<p><p>Optical fiber splice closures are used to protect and organize fiber optic cables. Dome type closures feature a dome-shaped design that allows for easy access to the fibers for maintenance and repairs. They are typically used in aerial installations. Horizontal type closures have a flat shape and are suitable for underground installations. They provide a compact and secure housing for the fiber splices. Both types of closures serve the same purpose of protecting and managing fiber optic cables, but they have different designs to suit different installation locations.</p></p>
