<p><p>Aluminum Fluoride salts are categorized based on their water content. Wet Aluminum Fluoride salts contain water molecules within their structure, while Dry Aluminum Fluoride salts are free of water. Anhydrous Aluminum Fluoride salts have no water molecules present. Each type has unique properties that make them suitable for different industrial applications. Wet salts are often used in industries requiring a liquid form, while Dry and Anhydrous salts are preferred for applications where moisture can cause issues or contamination.</p></p>
