<p><p>Water saving integrated toilets come in two main types - flush type and siphon type. Flush type toilets use a gravity-fed system to flush waste away with minimal water usage, making them more environmentally friendly. On the other hand, siphon type toilets use a siphoning action to remove waste, also reducing water consumption. Both types are designed to save water compared to traditional toilets while still maintaining efficient flushing performance, making them ideal for eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
