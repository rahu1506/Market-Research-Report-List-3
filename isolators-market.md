<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Loop Powered</li><li>Independent Powered</li><li>Output Loop Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isolators in the market can be categorized into three main types: Loop Powered, Independent Powered, and Output Loop Powered. Loop Powered isolators draw power from the current loop they are measuring, making them ideal for applications where external power sources are limited. Independent Powered isolators have their own power source and provide isolation without drawing power from the loop. Output Loop Powered isolators combine the features of loop powered and independent powered isolators by providing accurate measurements and isolation while drawing minimal power from the loop.</p></p>
