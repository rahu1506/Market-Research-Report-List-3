<p><p>Truck camshafts come in various types including cast camshafts, assembled camshafts, and forged camshafts. Cast camshafts are created by pouring molten metal into a mold, assembled camshafts are made by combining individual components, and forged camshafts are crafted by shaping metal under high pressure. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different performance needs. Cast camshafts are cost-effective, assembled camshafts offer customization options, and forged camshafts are known for their strength and durability.</p></p>
