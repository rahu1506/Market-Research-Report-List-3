<p><p>The hard drives market is divided into three main types: SSD (Solid State Drives), HDD (Mechanical Hard Disk), and HHD (Hybrid Hard Disk). SSDs are known for their fast read/write speeds and durability, making them ideal for high-performance computers. HDDs are traditional mechanical hard disks with larger storage capacities but slower speeds. HHDs combine the speed of SSDs with the larger storage capacity of HDDs, offering a balance between performance and storage space.</p></p>
