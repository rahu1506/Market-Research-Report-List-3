<p><p>Corrosion protection mastics are commonly categorized into two types: Rubberized Bitumen Based and Petrolatum Based. </p><p>Rubberized Bitumen Based mastics are thick, paste-like materials that contain a blend of rubber and bitumen, providing excellent adhesive properties and resistance to moisture. </p><p>Petrolatum Based mastics consist of a mixture of petrolatum and other additives, forming a thick, viscous material that effectively prevents corrosion by forming a protective barrier against water and other corrosive elements. </p><p>Both types of mastics are widely used in various industries for protecting metal surfaces from corrosion.</p></p>
