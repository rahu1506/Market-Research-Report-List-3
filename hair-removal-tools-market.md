<p><p>Hair removal tools market can be divided into three main types: epilators, laser, and IPL. Epilators are electronic devices that use rotating tweezers to pull hair from the root. Laser hair removal devices use a concentrated beam of light to target hair follicles and prevent regrowth. IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) devices work similarly to lasers but use a broader spectrum of light. Each type of tool offers a different method of hair removal, catering to individual preferences and needs.</p></p>
