<p><p>Electrolytic copper foil is available in different thickness ranges, including below 10 um, 10-20 um, 20-50 um, and above 50 um. Foils below 10 um are ideal for applications requiring high flexibility and conductivity, such as flexible electronics. Copper foils in the 10-20 um range are commonly used in printed circuit boards. Foils in the 20-50 um range are suitable for high-power applications, while those above 50 um are used in applications requiring high current carrying capacity, such as power electronics.</p></p>
