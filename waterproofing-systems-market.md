<p><p>Waterproofing systems market includes various types such as waterproofing membranes, coatings, and injection grouting. Waterproofing membranes are sheets or barriers applied to surfaces to prevent water penetration. Waterproofing coatings are liquid or semi-liquid materials used to create a protective layer against water. Injection grouting involves injecting grout materials into cracks or voids to seal off water ingress. Each type serves a specific purpose in protecting structures from water damage, offering solutions suited to different needs and conditions.</p></p>
