<p><p>Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB) come in different types based on their tripping characteristics: Type B, Type C, and Type D. Type B MCBs are suitable for circuits with low inrush currents, Type C MCBs are ideal for circuits with moderate inrush currents, and Type D MCBs are designed for circuits with high inrush currents. Each type offers specific protection levels, allowing for customized circuit protection based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
