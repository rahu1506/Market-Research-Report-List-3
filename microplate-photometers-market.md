<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microplate photometers are used for quantitative analysis of samples in microplates. There are two main types in the market: Optical Filter Microplate Readers and Optical Grating Microplate Readers. Optical Filter Microplate Readers use specific filters to measure absorbance at wavelengths corresponding to different analytes. On the other hand, Optical Grating Microplate Readers use diffraction grating technology to scan a range of wavelengths simultaneously. Both types offer high sensitivity and accuracy in measuring samples in microplates for research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
