<p><p>Automotive exhaust mufflers are essential components that help reduce noise levels and emissions produced by the vehicle's engine. The market is divided into two main types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers utilize sound-absorbing materials to dampen noise, while reactive mufflers use specially designed chambers and tubes to reflect sound waves and cancel out noise. Both types aim to provide a quieter and cleaner driving experience, catering to different preferences and requirements of vehicle owners.</p></p>
