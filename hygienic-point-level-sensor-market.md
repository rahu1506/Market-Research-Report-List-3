<p><strong>The Hygienic Point Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hygienic point level sensor market consists of two main types: contact type and non-contact type sensors. Contact type sensors physically come into contact with the substance being measured, providing accurate readings but requiring maintenance and cleaning. Non-contact type sensors use technologies such as ultrasonic, radar, or optical sensors to detect levels without touching the substance, offering minimal maintenance and easy integration but potentially less accuracy. Both types cater to different industries and applications based on the specific requirements for cleanliness and efficiency.</p></p>
