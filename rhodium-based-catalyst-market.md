<p><p>Rhodium-based catalysts are available in two main market types: powder and blocky. Powdered rhodium catalysts are finely ground particles that offer high surface area for catalytic reactions. They are commonly used in a variety of industrial applications due to their high reactivity. Blocky rhodium catalysts, on the other hand, are compacted into solid blocks or pellets, offering longer catalyst life and easier handling. They are often used in continuous processes where extended catalyst life is critical. Both powder and blocky rhodium catalysts play important roles in various catalytic reactions and processes.</p></p>
