<p><p>Transformer insulation paper market types can be classified into Nomex, DMD (Diamond Dotted Paper), NMN (Nomex-Mylar-Nomex), and other types. Nomex insulation paper is known for its high thermal stability and durability. DMD paper is reinforced with polyester film for enhanced mechanical strength. NMN paper combines the properties of Nomex and Mylar for improved insulation performance. Other types of insulation paper may include materials such as Kraft paper, pressboard, and crepe paper, each offering unique advantages in transformer applications.</p></p>
