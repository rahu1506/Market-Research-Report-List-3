<p><p>The self-levelling building material market includes two main types: underlayment and toppings. Underlayment is used to create a smooth and level surface for flooring installations, while toppings are a thin layer applied on top of concrete to enhance its appearance and durability. Underlayment is typically used in new construction or renovation projects, while toppings are more commonly used in decorative applications. Both types of materials help improve the overall quality and longevity of flooring systems in various residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
