<p><p>Companion barcode scanners are commonly categorized into two main types: 1D and 2D scanners. 1D scanners are traditional linear scanners that can only read one-dimensional barcodes, consisting of vertical black lines and spaces. On the other hand, 2D scanners can read two-dimensional barcodes like QR codes, which contain both horizontal and vertical patterns. 1D scanners are typically used for basic inventory and retail applications, while 2D scanners are more versatile and can be used for various industries such as healthcare, logistics, and ticketing.</p></p>
