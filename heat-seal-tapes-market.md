<p><p>Heat seal tapes are used in various industries for packaging and sealing purposes. The market for heat seal tapes can be categorized into three main types - PTFE type, TPU type, and PI type. PTFE type heat seal tapes are known for their non-stick properties, making them ideal for use in food packaging. TPU type heat seal tapes are popular for their flexibility and durability, commonly used in textile and medical industries. PI type heat seal tapes offer high temperature resistance and chemical stability, making them suitable for advanced industrial applications.</p></p>
