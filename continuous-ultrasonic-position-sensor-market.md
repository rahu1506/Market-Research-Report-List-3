<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Retro-Reflective Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Through Beam Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor Market Types include Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors, Ultrasonic Retro-Reflective Sensors, and Ultrasonic Through Beam Sensors. Proximity sensors detect the presence of an object without physical contact. Retro-Reflective sensors use a reflector to bounce back the ultrasonic waves to the sensor. Through Beam sensors have a separate transmitter and receiver to detect an object when it breaks the beam. These sensors are used in various applications for accurate distance measurement and position detection in industrial automation and robotics.</p></p>
