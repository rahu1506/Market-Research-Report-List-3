<p><p>Brass valves market consists of various types such as gate valves, globe valves, ball valves, check valves, and others. Gate valves are primarily used to control the flow of liquid, while globe valves regulate the flow with a disk moving up and down. Ball valves use a perforated ball to control flow, and check valves only allow flow in one direction. Other types of brass valves may include butterfly valves, diaphragm valves, and pinch valves, each serving different purposes in fluid control systems.</p></p>
