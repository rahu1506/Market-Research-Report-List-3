<p><p>Lead smelting and refining involves extracting lead from its ores and purifying it through various methods. The market types for lead smelting and refining include pyrometallurgical, solvent extraction, and ion exchange processes. Pyrometallurgical involves heating the ores to high temperatures to separate lead from other minerals. Solvent extraction uses organic solvents to selectively extract lead from the ore. Ion exchange involves exchanging lead ions with other ions to purify the metal. Each process has its own advantages and applications in the lead industry.</p></p>
