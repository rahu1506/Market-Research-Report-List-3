<p><strong>The Elliptical Trainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Drive</li><li>Rear Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elliptical trainer market can be grouped into three main types based on the drive system: front drive, rear drive, and center drive. Front drive ellipticals have the flywheel located at the front of the machine, providing a smooth and natural-feeling stride. Rear drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned at the back, offering a more compact design and often a more intense workout. Center drive ellipticals place the flywheel in the middle, promoting a more balanced and stable exercise experience. Each type caters to different preferences and workout styles.</p></p>
