<p><p>Automotive Automatic Transmission Control Units come in two main types: Hardwired Control Units and Microprogrammable Control Units. Hardwired Control Units have specific circuits and components that are physically connected and programmed to control the transmission. On the other hand, Microprogrammable Control Units use software to control the transmission, allowing for more flexibility and customization. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient operation of automatic transmissions in vehicles.</p></p>
