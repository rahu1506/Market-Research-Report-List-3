<p><p>Near Infrared Medical Imaging encompasses two main types: Near Infrared Fluorescence Imaging Devices and Near Infrared Bioluminescence Imaging Devices. Near Infrared Fluorescence Imaging Devices use fluorescence agents to produce image contrast, aiding in the visualization of specific molecules in biological tissues. On the other hand, Near Infrared Bioluminescence Imaging Devices use bioluminescent enzymes to emit light and enable the tracking of cellular and molecular processes in living organisms. Both technologies have significant applications in medical diagnostics and research.</p></p>
