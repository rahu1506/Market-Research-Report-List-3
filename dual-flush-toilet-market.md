<p><p>The Dual Flush Toilet market consists of three main types: Ball Valve Toilet, Self-controlled Water-saving Toilet, and Pneumatic Toilet. Ball Valve Toilets use a mechanism that controls the release of water for flushing. Self-controlled Water-saving Toilets have a built-in system that allows users to choose between a full or partial flush. Pneumatic Toilets use air pressure to regulate the flow of water for flushing. These different types cater to varying preferences and needs for water conservation in the market.</p></p>
