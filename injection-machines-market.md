<p><p>Injection machines are classified into different types based on their structure and design. Vertical type injection machines have a vertical clamping unit and injection unit, making them suitable for products with height restrictions. Horizontal type injection machines have a horizontal clamping unit and injection unit, ideal for products that require a larger mold size. Other types of injection machines may include hybrid machines or all-electric machines, offering different benefits such as energy efficiency, precision, and speed. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
