Pneumatic pumps use compressed air to operate, offering a quieter alternative to electric pumps. Electromagnetic pumps operate using electromagnetic forces, reducing noise levels even further. Each type of pump offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications based on noise level requirements.</p></p>
