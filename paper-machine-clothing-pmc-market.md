<p><ul><li>Forming Fabrics</li><li>Press Felts</li><li>Dryer Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paper Machine Clothing (PMC) market consists of three main types: forming fabrics, press felts, and dryer fabrics. Forming fabrics are used in the initial stage of papermaking to drain and form the sheet. Press felts are used in the press section to remove water from the sheet. Dryer fabrics are used to support the sheet as it passes through heated rollers to remove the remaining moisture. These components play a crucial role in the papermaking process by ensuring proper drainage, pressing, and drying of the sheet.</p></p>
